Well met, young adventurerand welcome to the peaceful realm of Silven Valley.

Actually, it used to be peaceful until the Wumpus, a vile creature, took up residence in caverns nearby.It has been terrorising the villages all along the valley for a long time now.

So, the elders from the villages have banded together to offer fameand fortune to the worthy adventurer who will rid their valley of this dreaded monster!

You have answered the calland have been told by the elders that the creature is lurking in one of the cavern complexes in the surrounding hills.

All you have to do is find the creatureand slay it ... simple!
